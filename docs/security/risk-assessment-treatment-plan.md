# Risk Assessment and Treatment Plan

## Executive Summary

This document outlines the information security risk assessment for the Chiral Infrastructure as Code platform, conducted in accordance with ISO/IEC 27001:2022 requirements.

## Scope

The assessment covers:
- Information assets
- Threats and vulnerabilities
- Risk levels
- Treatment plans

## Methodology

Risk assessment methodology based on ISO/IEC 27005:
- Asset identification
- Threat identification
- Vulnerability assessment
- Impact analysis
- Risk calculation

## Key Risks Identified

### 1. Unauthorized Access
- **Likelihood**: Medium
- **Impact**: High
- **Current Controls**: RBAC, MFA
- **Treatment**: Implement zero-trust architecture

### 2. Data Breach
- **Likelihood**: Low
- **Impact**: Critical
- **Current Controls**: Encryption, monitoring
- **Treatment**: Enhance incident response

### 3. Supply Chain Attack
- **Likelihood**: Medium
- **Impact**: High
- **Current Controls**: Dependency scanning
- **Treatment**: Implement software bill of materials

### 4. Cloud Provider Incident
- **Likelihood**: Low
- **Impact**: High
- **Current Controls**: Multi-region deployment
- **Treatment**: Business continuity planning

## Risk Treatment Plan

### Risk Acceptance
- Low impact risks accepted with monitoring

### Risk Mitigation
- Implement additional controls for medium/high risks

### Risk Transfer
- Cybersecurity insurance for catastrophic events

### Risk Avoidance
- Discontinue high-risk practices

## Monitoring and Review

- Quarterly risk assessments
- Annual review
- Continuous monitoring via SIEM
